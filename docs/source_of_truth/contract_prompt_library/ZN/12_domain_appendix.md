# Domain Appendix

## Core driver stack
- Fed-path expectations
- inflation versus growth split
- term premium and supply/issuance
- risk-off demand
- curve steepening or flattening context

## Critical discipline
- Keep mechanisms internally consistent.
- Downgrade narrative certainty when the market has likely priced the information already.
- Separate context from catalyst and catalyst from executable trigger.
- Always include the section HOW THIS SHOULD INFORM TRADING, NOT DICTATE TRADING.

## Common misuse
using article narratives to override clear yield rejection, auction failure, or curve behavior that points to a different mechanism than the headline suggests
