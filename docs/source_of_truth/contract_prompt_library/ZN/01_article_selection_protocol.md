# Article Selection Protocol

Select only articles that have a plausible pathway into 10-Year Treasury Note Futures (ZN). Prioritize new information, primary reporting, policy-significant analysis, macro data interpretation, and articles that can plausibly change how the market prices the contract.

## Include first
- primary reporting with new information
- policy, macro, or market-structure articles directly tied to Fed-path repricing, inflation-growth decomposition, curve context, auctions, issuance, fiscal credibility, and term premium
- clusters where multiple outlets are forcing a narrative onto the same move
- pieces that may change the timing, durability, or mechanism of the market response

## Down-rank
- generic opinion columns
- pure recap articles with no new facts
- emotional or thematic narratives unsupported by market mechanism
- articles whose relevance cannot be traced to the contract in one or two clean steps

## Selection rubric
Score each candidate article from 1 to 5 on:
1. novelty
2. source quality
3. contract relevance
4. timing sensitivity
5. explanatory mechanism clarity

Only push articles into the deep-analysis stage when the combined score suggests actual decision value, not merely intellectual interest.
