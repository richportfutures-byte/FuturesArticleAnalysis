# Narrative Clustering and Screening

When several articles discuss the same move or event, cluster them before deep analysis.

## Workflow
1. Group articles by event, policy catalyst, or price move.
2. Identify what each article claims is *new*.
3. Separate the dominant narrative from article-specific additions.
4. Flag where writers are describing the same market move with different causes.
5. Mark whether the cluster reflects discovery, consensus, or post hoc storytelling.

## Screening outputs
- common facts across the cluster
- disputed causal claims
- strongest article by evidence quality
- weakest article by unsupported narrative reach
- whether the cluster is likely actionable, informational, or noise

## Contract-specific screen
For 10-Year Treasury Note Futures (ZN), reject clusters that do not clearly route through at least one of these channels:
- Fed-path expectations
- inflation versus growth split
- term premium and supply/issuance
- risk-off demand
- curve steepening or flattening context
