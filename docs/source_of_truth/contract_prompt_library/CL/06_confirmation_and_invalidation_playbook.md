# Confirmation and Invalidation Playbook

## Confirmation markers
Use a small set of cross-checks rather than one headline:
- front-month crude price acceptance and follow-through
- term-structure response: prompt strength versus deferred contracts
- Brent-WTI behavior when relevant
- EIA/API inventories and key subcomponents when the thesis is inventory-related
- refinery utilization, runs, and outage confirmation when the thesis is refinery-led
- crack-spread behavior when product pull-through is central
- tanker, shipping, sanctions, or chokepoint follow-through when the thesis is logistics-led
- dollar behavior and broad risk sentiment when the article is macro-linked

## Invalidation markers
- price rejects the narrative quickly and cannot hold the headline zone
- the curve does not confirm a supposedly tight physical story
- products fail to confirm a refinery or demand thesis
- official data contradicts the article's stated mechanism
- the move looks large but breadth of energy confirmation is poor

## Required output section
Every analysis must end with:
- what would confirm the thesis
- what would invalidate it
- whether the article should bias you toward continuation, fade, wait, or no trade
