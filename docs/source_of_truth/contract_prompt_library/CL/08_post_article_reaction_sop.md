# Post-Article Reaction SOP

After a crude-related article hits and the market moves:

1. Identify the first actual new fact.
2. Distinguish primary reporting from follow-on commentary.
3. Measure whether the move is larger than the factual novelty justifies.
4. Check whether the curve, products, and related benchmarks agree.
5. Decide whether the move is:
   - durable repricing
   - temporary headline shock
   - narrative attachment after a technical move
   - mixed and not cleanly tradable
6. Translate the result into one of four instructions for yourself:
   - bias continuation setups
   - bias fade setups
   - wait for confirmation
   - ignore

## Guardrail
Never treat the article alone as sufficient proof that the move should continue.
