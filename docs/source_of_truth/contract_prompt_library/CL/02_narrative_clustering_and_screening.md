# Narrative Clustering and Screening

When multiple articles appear around the same move, cluster them into narrative buckets before analysis.

## Mandatory buckets
- supply shock or disruption
- OPEC+ policy or quota enforcement
- inventory and storage narrative
- refinery or product-market narrative
- growth-demand narrative
- dollar or macro-liquidity narrative
- geopolitical premium narrative
- post hoc explanation or low-information commentary

## Screening questions
1. Are multiple articles actually describing the same underlying catalyst?
2. Which article contains the newest verifiable information?
3. Which articles are inference layered on top of the first report?
4. Is the market move consistent with the strongest article or is the media overfitting the move?
5. Does the narrative belong in crude itself, in products, or in a different market entirely?

## Output requirement
Return a ranked list of narratives with:
- strongest source article
- confidence that the narrative is actually new
- whether the narrative is likely tradable, context only, or noise
