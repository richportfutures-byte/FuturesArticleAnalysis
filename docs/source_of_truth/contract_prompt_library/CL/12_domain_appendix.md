# Domain Appendix

## Core driver stack
- OPEC+ policy, cohesion, and compliance
- physical disruption and restoration risk
- inventories, storage, and balance trajectory
- refinery utilization, outages, and maintenance
- product pull-through and crack-spread behavior
- sanctions, shipping, and chokepoint logistics
- global growth demand and macro regime
- US dollar and broad risk sentiment

## Critical discipline
- Separate crude-specific effects from product-specific effects.
- Distinguish prompt physical tightness from longer-dated narrative drift.
- Downgrade certainty when the move can be explained better by positioning than by new facts.
- Separate context from catalyst and catalyst from executable trigger.
- Always include the section HOW THIS SHOULD INFORM TRADING, NOT DICTATE TRADING.

## Common misuse
Buying or selling crude off a dramatic geopolitical headline without checking whether the curve, products, and physical-balance indicators actually confirm the story.
