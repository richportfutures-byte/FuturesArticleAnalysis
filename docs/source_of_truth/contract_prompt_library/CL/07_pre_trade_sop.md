# Pre-Trade SOP

Before using the workflow output in live decision-making, run this checklist:

1. Is the article actually relevant to crude, or is it more relevant to products, equities, rates, or FX?
2. Is the article new, or is it explaining a move that already happened?
3. What is the dominant mechanism: supply, demand, inventory, refinery, geopolitics, shipping, or macro?
4. Does the prompt-month and curve response support the story?
5. Are products and related energy benchmarks confirming or diverging?
6. Does price action accept the narrative or reject it?
7. Is this a continuation setup, fade setup, or no-trade condition?
8. What evidence would force you to abandon the thesis?

## Operating rule
If you cannot answer these clearly, downgrade conviction and do not let the workflow output become a permission slip.
