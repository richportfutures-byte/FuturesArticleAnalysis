# Domain Appendix

## Core driver stack
- ECB versus Fed rate-spread expectations
- Europe versus US growth differential
- inflation differential and policy credibility
- energy and trade-balance sensitivity
- broad dollar regime versus euro-specific repricing

## Critical discipline
- Keep mechanisms internally consistent.
- Downgrade narrative certainty when the market has likely priced the information already.
- Separate context from catalyst and catalyst from executable trigger.
- Always include the section HOW THIS SHOULD INFORM TRADING, NOT DICTATE TRADING.

## Common misuse
using generic risk-on or risk-off commentary as a euro signal when the article does not clearly alter relative policy or growth expectations
