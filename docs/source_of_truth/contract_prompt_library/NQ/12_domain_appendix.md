# Domain Appendix

## Core driver stack
- US rates and real yields
- megacap tech leadership
- semiconductors and AI capex
- broad risk sentiment and liquidity
- earnings and growth expectations

## Critical discipline
- Keep mechanisms internally consistent.
- Downgrade narrative certainty when the market has likely priced the information already.
- Separate context from catalyst and catalyst from executable trigger.
- Always include the section HOW THIS SHOULD INFORM TRADING, NOT DICTATE TRADING.

## Common misuse
single-article trading during microstructure-driven chop, dealer-gamma pinning, or when article logic conflicts with clear breadth and leadership deterioration
