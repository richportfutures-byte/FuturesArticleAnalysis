# Post-Article Reaction SOP

After the market reacts, answer:
1. Did the contract move in the direction implied by the article?
2. Did the relevant cross-market signals confirm the mechanism?
3. Was the move sustained, faded, or fully reversed?
4. Does the article now look like a catalyst, a rationalization, or irrelevant noise?
5. Should the event be logged as continuation fuel, fade fuel, or future ignore material?

Keep a review log so the workflow improves as a practical operator tool rather than remaining a purely analytical exercise.
