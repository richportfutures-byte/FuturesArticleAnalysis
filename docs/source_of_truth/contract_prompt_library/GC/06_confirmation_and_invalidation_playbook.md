# Confirmation and Invalidation Playbook

## Confirmation markers for Gold Futures (GC / MGC)
- real yields versus nominal yields
- DXY confirmation or contradiction
- Treasury reaction
- oil and commodity spillover
- spot gold behavior across the US session

## Invalidation logic
Invalidate or downgrade the workflow output when:
- the supposed transmission channel fails to appear in the relevant cross-market markers
- the contract reacts in the opposite direction and then gains acceptance there
- the price move looks technical while the supposed macro mechanism remains absent
- later information weakens the article's core novelty or factual basis

## Trade-use rule
A workflow output can justify *looking for* setups that align with the thesis. It cannot justify forcing a trade in the absence of confirmation.
