# Domain Appendix

## Core driver stack
- real-yield direction
- DXY and broad dollar regime
- central-bank and reserve-diversification demand
- geopolitical premium
- inflation expectations and crisis hedging

## Critical discipline
- Keep mechanisms internally consistent.
- Downgrade narrative certainty when the market has likely priced the information already.
- Separate context from catalyst and catalyst from executable trigger.
- Always include the section HOW THIS SHOULD INFORM TRADING, NOT DICTATE TRADING.

## Common misuse
blindly buying or selling gold off geopolitical headlines without cross-asset confirmation or when liquidation pressure dominates safe-haven logic
